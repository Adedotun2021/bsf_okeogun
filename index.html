Evangelism Units

The main objective of evangelism is to allow people who have not heard -- or have not yet accepted -- the gospel of Christ the opportunity to hear the gospel and respond to it. Christians often cite St. Paul, who said, "How can people have faith in the Lord and ask him to save them, if they have never heard about him? And how can they hear, unless someone tells them?" (Romans 10:14, Contemporary English Version, or CEV) Christians hope those who hear the message will come to faith in Christ and become Christians.

Media Units

We keep records of sermons and events in Church, notify people about Church programs, broadcast the sermons to people outside the church, project Bible's and songs during church programs manage the church pages on internet.

Drama Units

The unit is about displaying and interpreting to God through our bisual display, drama , and equipping men by the word of God through the use of stage play

Organizing Units

The Organizing unit organizes, beautifies, adores and honours, a place (the church) with creativity. As a fellowship organizer, we also entrust ourselves to minister the gospel with our organizations, preparations And decorations using proper wiring, arrangements, coordination and colours and fabrics.

Ushering Units

This is a unit of the Church where we usher people in and out of the church. We help to direct members of the Church, visitors and officiating ministers for spiritual worship, ensure orderliness during the worship sessions as well as cleaning and arranging the chairs before the commencement of any worship service.
